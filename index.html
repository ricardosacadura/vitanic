About
Data visualization regarding the Titanic ship disaster in 1912, we wanted to create an artifact that translates data oriented to users with a historical interest in the event. The goal was to investigate relationships between different types of data, for example how many citizens survived having traveled 1st class, or what proportion of children was saved in relation to adults aged 30-45.
